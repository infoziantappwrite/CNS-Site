Frequently Asked Questions.

Our dedicated customer support team is here to help you with any questions or concerns. We are committed to providing you with the assistance you need, whenever you need it.

How do I become an affiliate?
Joining our affiliate program is easy! Simply sign up on our website, provide some basic information, and you'll receive a unique affiliate link to start promoting.
What are the benefits of joining your affiliate program?
As an affiliate, you'll earn commissions on every sale made through your links. You'll also gain access to exclusive promotional materials, support, and training.
How and when do I get paid?
We process payments on a monthly basis. You'll receive your earnings via bank transfer, PayPal, or another preferred payment method once you reach the minimum payout threshold.
