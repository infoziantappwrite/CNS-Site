Cleaning & Maintenance

Clean Spaces, Healthy Living

Introduction

Consistent Cleanliness, Continuous Care

Our Cleaning & Maintenance services are designed to uphold hygiene, comfort, and aesthetic appeal across residential, commercial, and institutional spaces. From spotless interiors to pest-free premises and green landscapes, Flib ensures your environment is well cared for—every day.

Housekeeping Services
Daily or scheduled cleaning services for homes, offices, and buildings—covering floor cleaning, dusting, washroom sanitation, and trash management.
Pest Management
Safe and effective pest control solutions for cockroaches, rodents, termites, mosquitoes, and more—using certified, eco-friendly products.
Garden & Lawn Maintenance
Regular upkeep of green spaces through mowing, trimming, fertilizing, watering, and pest care to keep your landscapes fresh and healthy.
Deep Cleaning Services
Comprehensive cleaning of floors, kitchens, washrooms, and corners using industrial-grade tools and safe chemicals—ideal for post-renovation or seasonal refresh.
Sofa & Carpet Shampooing
Deep fabric cleaning for upholstered furniture and carpets to remove stains, dust mites, and allergens—leaving your interiors fresh and hygienic.
Waste Disposal Management
Segregation, collection, and responsible disposal of general, dry, wet, and recyclable waste in compliance with local sanitation rules.

Our Process

Clean. Safe. Well-Maintained.

Step 1: We assess your site to identify specific cleaning or maintenance needs.

Step 2: A routine or on-call schedule is created, and expert staff are assigned to the tasks.

Step 3: Tasks are executed with quality checks and eco-conscious practices for reliable, lasting results.
